Help - Personal Budget Guide

Introduction

Welcome to the Personal Budget Tracker! This tool helps you manage your income, track expenses, and stay within your budget for categories like Food, Travel, Shopping, and Jewellery.

How to Use

  • Dashboard: View overall summary with bar and pie charts.
  • Employees: Add/update income/expenses, view employee history.
  • Budget: Set and view monthly/weekly budgets by category.
  • Reports: Visualize income/expenses trends and breakdowns.

Common Actions

  • Add a new income or expense record.
  • Update or delete an existing record.
  • Filter reports by date or category.
  • Set monthly/weekly budget limits by category.

FAQs

Q: Can I edit submitted records?
A: Yes, go to the Employees tab and click "Update" on the desired entry.

Q: How to view category-wise expenses?
A: Go to the Reports section and select the category.

Q: What happens if I exceed my budget?
A: The app will still allow entries but notify you when you've exceeded your set budget.

Q: Can I add past transactions?
A: Yes, you can manually select any date while adding a new income/expense.

Q: Who can see my budget data?
A: Only you and authorized admins can view or modify your personal budget data.

Q: How do I delete a budget?
A: Go to the Budget section and click the delete button next to the desired category.
